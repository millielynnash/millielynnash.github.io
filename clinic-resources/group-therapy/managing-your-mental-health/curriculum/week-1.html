Week 1

Paperwork

  • Assist clients in completing all outstanding paperwork
  • Administer symptom checklist
  • Review informed consent
  • Review treatment agreement
    • Confidentiality
      • What is said in group stays in group, unless you said it.
      • Can talk about your own stuff in group, but no one else and no names.
  • Mandated reporting
  • Any additional suggested rules from the group?

Introduction

  • Create name tags with pronouns
  • Facilitate introductions
    • Name, employment/school status, current living situation, a funny fact, something they do well, and something they do for fun.
  • Introduce ourselves

Icebreaker (Cross the Line Activity)

  • Facilitate the Cross the Line Activity
    • What kind of feelings did you have as you participated?
    • How did you feel when there were fewer of you on one side of the room?
    • How did the movement of others influence you?
    • What did you learn through this activity that can make our group more welcoming?

Introduction to the Group

  • Explore and Process
    • What is mental health?
    • Why does mental health matter?
    • What brings you to the group?
    • What are you hoping to get out of the group?

Today's Skill: Mindfulness

  • Mindfulness - Observing a Piece of Fruit
  • Mindfulness of Taste
    • Explore how and when this skill might be helpgul outside of group.

Past Experiences in Therapy (If There is Time)

  • Explore past experiences in therapy, specifically group therapy.

Wrap Up, Reactions, and Take Aways

  • Process reactions to participating in the group.
    • What was today like for you?
    • Was anything surprising or new for you?
    • What are you taking away?

Additional Resources

  • DBT Mindfulness Skills | MARSHA LINEHAN - YouTube
  • Mindfulness of Current Emotion - Marsha on Vimeo
