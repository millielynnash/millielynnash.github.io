One-Way ANOVA

AspectDetails
PurposeTests for mean differences in a Continuous Dependent Variable across two or more groups (one Independent factor)
Example Use CaseExamining differences in exam scores between three different teaching methods.
Key Assumptions- Independence: Participants belong to only one group (ensured by study design).
- Normality: The dependent variable is normally distributed within each group (Shapiro–Wilk p > .05).
- Homogeneity of Variance: The variance of each group is similar (Levene’s Test p > .05).
