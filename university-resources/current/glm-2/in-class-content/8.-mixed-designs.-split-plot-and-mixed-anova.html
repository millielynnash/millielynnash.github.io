Overview of Mixed Designs

  • We call designs 'mixed' when a design includes Independent variables that are measured using different entities (groups/conditions) and others that include repeated measures.
    • Also referred to as 'split-plot'
  • Includes both within-subject and between-subject factors
  • Can also mean fixed-factors and random-factors
